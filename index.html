Where was I..? Ah yes, I am actually a hamster - a golden hamster, also called Syrian hamster because I have my roots there.
There are several types of hamsters out there, but I am the biggest one! Ok, except for the ones livin' out in nature, they are bigger... but not as cute as I am!

Anyways, there are many things people dunno 'bout hamsters and how to take care of 'em.

For example, I am not a suitable pet for kids. I need ma sleep during the day, and kids tend to be quite noisy..
I am active when kids are sleeping, so they don't see one bit of me. And because of that they try to wake me up during the day which in turn stresses me out. By the end of the day, I will probably - no surely - die sooner. And I don't even live that long... my life expectancy is only around 2 human years.. So, please make sure to give ma fellow hamsters a wonderful life.

To help every hamster have a happy and healthy life, ma human made this page to inform you about how to take care of a lil fella like me.
